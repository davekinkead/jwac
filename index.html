JWAC Analysis & Visualisation

What is the best way to understand and analyse training flows? The introduction of NGD and IE9 permits new technologies and tools to assist with this.

One new opportunity is to use single page apps that read data exported from PMkeys and allow visual exploration. Below are some examples to illustrate what is possible.

Dashboard

Dashboards provide an instant of key data and metrics. This one shows JWAC KPIs for each year.

Structural Analysis

The JWAC structural analysis shows the flow of trainees through the JWAC training pipeline. Based on individual posting history, the graph aggregates cohorts into one single directed graph.

Double click any training gate to filter only JOUTs that pass through that gate.

Member Analysis

This visualisation shows the progress of individual JOUTs grouped by cohort. Historical data is applied to estimate the likely BWC completion date.

Sensitivity Analysis

The sensitivity analysis shows what happens to the historical training structure if parameters change.

Double click any course to alter numbers.
